About Rotaract District 3120

Rotaract International District 3120 was formed in 1984-85 after the bifurcation of District 311 into 3110 and 3120. Covering 32 districts of Eastern Uttar Pradesh and 4 districts of Madhya Pradesh's Vindhya Plateau region, it lies just below Nepal in northern India. The district is home to over 80 Rotary Clubs and 28+ active Rotaract Clubs. Rotaract District 3120 is a vibrant tapestry of India’s ancient traditions, spiritual power centers, and cultural magnificence. The district is not only geographically vast but also culturally layered, reflecting the soul of Bharat.

Our Mission

Together we are on a mission to:

  • Mutual Empowerment & Growth
  • Expand Membership & Vibrant Network
  • Engage, Collaborate & Unite
  • Promote Learning & Professional Excellency
  • Promote Rotary’s 7 Areas of Focus
  • Elevate & Enhance Our District Groups
  • Explore the best of Rotary & Rotaract
  • Champion initiatives with Impact & Sustainability

We are together for Strengthening Humanity with Action, Knowledge, Teamwork, and Integrity.
